About us

Digital Beehive is a solution to empower smallholder famers and sustainable communities in developing countries by promoting accessible digital technology, spatial analytics and geographical intelligence in the palm of their hands.

Understand the Challenge

The Challenge

Digital Beehive is addressing Global Challenges at the local scale contributing towards resilient and sustainable communities.

Economics

Low rentability,
Inadequate incentives for innovation,
Lack of access to technologies.

Social

Absence of social cohesion,
Abandonment of agricultural practices,
Young farmers out-migration.

Environmental

Lack of production diversity,
Vulnerability to climate change,
Land degradation and overuse of chemicals.
