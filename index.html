About us

Digital Beehive is a solution to empower smallholder famers and sustainable communities in developing countries by promoting accessible digital technology, spatial analytics and geographical intelligence in the palm of their hands.

Our Solution

Bee Keeping

High aggregate value and several ecological benefits. Apicultural productivity is determined by the land use, pollination area, distance between hives, among other factors.

Digital Platform

Digital Beehive is a tool to support beekeepers planning the identification of optimal location and quantity of beehives in a given territory.

Predicting and Planning

The platform also provides automatic land use mapping, thus quantifying the bee-pasture and determining its productive potential, foreseeing the economic feasibility and sustainability of beekeeping practices.
